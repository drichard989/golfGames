🏦 Banker

Scoring Mode
How It Works

One player is the Banker per hole. Banker sets max bet. Other players bet (min to max). Players can double after hitting. Banker can double all bets after hitting. Lower score wins.

Legend
Banker: Player taking bets on this hole
Max Bet: Highest amount other players can bet
Bets: Each player's bet amount + double option (2×)
Banker 2×: Banker doubles all outstanding bets
Gross: Raw scores without handicap adjustment
Net: Scores adjusted by each player's raw handicap (+ handicaps add strokes)

Hi-Lo

How It Works: Requires exactly 4 players. Teams are auto-assigned: Team A = Lowest + Highest handicap, Team B = Middle two handicaps. Strokes are equalized by giving the difference to the worst player on the team with the lower combined handicap.

Scoring: Each hole awards 2 points (1 for low ball, 1 for high ball). Compare each team's lowest score for 1 point, then compare each team's highest score for 1 point. Ties = no points awarded.

Games: Three main games run simultaneously: Front 9 (holes 1-9, worth 1 unit), Back 9 (holes 10-18, worth 1 unit), Full 18 (all holes, worth 2 units) = 4 total units at stake.

Auto-Press: When a team wins a hole 2-0 (both low and high), an automatic press starts on the next hole. Front 9 presses run through hole 9, Back 9 presses run through hole 18. Each press is worth 1 unit (Full 18 has no presses). The leading team does NOT need to approve - presses trigger automatically!

🐺 Wolf

Scoring Mode
How It Works

Requires 4 players. Each hole a different player is the Wolf (rotates P1→P4). The Wolf picks a Partner (2v2) or plays alone as Lone Wolf (1v3), or declares Blind Wolf before anyone tees off (1v3, doubled). Lower team ball wins the hole; ties push.

Scoring
Partner win: +1 each partner.
Partner loss: +1 each opponent.
Lone Wolf wins: +3 to Wolf.
Lone Wolf loses: +1 each opponent.
Blind Wolf wins: +4 to Wolf.
Blind Wolf loses: +2 each opponent.

Wolf requires exactly 4 players. Add or remove players on the scorecard.

Hole Wolf Decision Result

Net Double Bogey per hole: cap = Par + 2 + strokes received on that hole. Players "play off" the lowest CH (e.g., +2 / 2 / 4 → adjusted 0 / 4 / 6).
